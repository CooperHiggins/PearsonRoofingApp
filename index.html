Welcome to Pearson Roofing

Pearson Roofing has decades of experience in repairing, installing and replacing commercial roofing. We have the technical skills and equipment to handle your commercial roofing replacement. You might not know that there are many different types of commercial roofing styles to choose from. Low slope, steep grade, and flat roofs are common for commercial buildings. We've had the pleasure of working on many commercial projects in DFW as well as other states throughout the US. From Multi-family housing, churches, and government projects, we have the expertise and always deliver the highest quality of work!
